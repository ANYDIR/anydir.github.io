Official formats

DJ Hero has an extremely wide variety of formats within it. The majority of these formats can be decompressed and decoded using DJHTools by Shockdude or are standard formats.

Formats

  • XMK and FSGMUB

    XMK and FSGMUB are chart formats for DJ Hero 2 and DJ Hero respectively. The only differences between FSGMUB and XMK are some of the note IDs.
