# Official formats

DJ Hero has an extremely wide variety of formats within it. The majority of these formats can be decompressed and decoded using [DJHTools](https://anydir.github.io/tools/djhtools/) by Shockdude or are standard formats.

## Formats

* [__XMK and FSGMUB__](https://anydir.github.io/formats/official/xmkandfsgmub/)

    XMK and FSGMUB are chart formats for DJ Hero 2 and DJ Hero respectively. The only differences between FSGMUB and XMK are some of the note IDs.
